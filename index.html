About Us

A Commitment to Quality Healthcare

Rudraksh Hospital is a modern multi-speciality healthcare institution located near Sankat Mochan in Varanasi. Established in 2022, the hospital is committed to providing quality medical care through experienced healthcare professionals, modern diagnostic facilities, and patient-focused treatment approaches.

As an ISO 9001:2015 certified hospital and an Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY empanelled healthcare provider, Rudraksh Hospital strives to make quality healthcare accessible and affordable for all sections of society.

The hospital offers a wide range of medical specialties and advanced healthcare services designed to meet the needs of patients with compassion, professionalism, and excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the hospital timings?

Our official consultation timings are Monday to Sunday, 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. However, please call ahead to confirm specific doctor availability.

Yes, we are proudly empanelled under the Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY scheme. Eligible patients can avail of cashless treatment up to ₹5 Lakhs.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through the form on our website, by directly messaging us on WhatsApp at 7007098548, or by calling our reception numbers.
